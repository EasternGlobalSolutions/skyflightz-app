Services

Our Services

WorldWide Travel

SkyFlightz partner with airlines worldwide, offering access to flights to virtually any destination with commercial airports. We cover major cities, remote locations, and international hubs, ensuring travelers can travel almost anywhere on the globe.

Travel Insurance

Offer travelers the choice to purchase travel insurance covering trip cancellations, medical emergencies abroad, lost luggage, and other unforeseen circumstances. Seamlessly integrate insurance options into the booking process, allowing users to add insurance with just a few clicks.

Flight Search and Comparison

SkyFlightz provide a user-friendly experience for travelers to search for flights based on their preferred dates, destinations, and other criteria. Enable comparison of prices, airlines, layovers, and travel times.

Customer Support

Provide round-the-clock customer service through various channels, including phone, email, live chat, and social media platforms. Offer specialized assistance for booking inquiries, flight changes, cancellations, refunds, and general travel advice.

Booking Management

We book the flights for users directly, ensuring the safety of personal and payment information. We also take care of the seat selection preferences of travelers during the booking process, such as window or aisle seats. We provide amends for the users to their bookings, such as rescheduling flights or adding extra services (e.g., baggage allowance, meal preferences). Skyflightz facilitate easy cancellation of bookings with transparent refund policies, displaying applicable fees or penalties.

Special Offers and Deals

Feature discounted fares, flash sales, and exclusive offers on flights to popular destinations. Bundle flights with hotel accommodations, car rentals, or activities, providing cost-effective travel packages for customers.

Flight Alerts and Notifications

Send real-time updates on flight status, including delays, gate changes, and cancellations. Notify travelers about upcoming check-in times and procedures, ensuring they are well-prepared for their flights. Provide information on baggage claim procedures and carousel numbers upon arrival at the destination.

Frequent Flyer Programs

Allow travelers to earn frequent flyer miles or points when booking flights through the website. Enable users to redeem accumulated miles or points for flight upgrades, free tickets, or other rewards offered by partner airlines.
